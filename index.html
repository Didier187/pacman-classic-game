Welcome to poor man's Pacman

How to play

  • use arrow-keys to move Pacman
  • Eat as many pacdots as you can this will earn you a point
  • Avoid running into ghosts [The four bright colored squares]
  • If you meet a ghost, the game is over
  • If Pacman eats a green pellet this will earn you 10 points and disable the ghost for a few seconds
